Earned Settlement: keeping momentum behind negotiated outcomes

Earned settlement insists that closing language is only the midpoint of client service. The goal is to demonstrate how every concession, warranty, and timetable will be honoured across teams once the documents are signed.

In practice, that means exhausting the detail early. I am currently mapping each phase of settlement implementation:

  • Capturing facts and authority boundaries before offers shift.
  • Translating headline numbers into live cash-flow and risk dashboards.
  • Preparing playbooks so solicitors, counsel, and client stakeholders give the same message under pressure.
  • Monitoring obligations post-signature with check-ins, shared trackers, and escalation routes.

The finished piece will include downloadable templates and debrief prompts. Until then, view this as a working outline and feel free to reach out if you want to compare approaches.
