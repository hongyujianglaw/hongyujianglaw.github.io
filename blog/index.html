About this blog

I write about moments that shaped how I understand law in practice.

These posts are based on real experiences — from navigating unfamiliar legal systems, to volunteering with advice services, to thinking carefully about where technology helps and where it should not.

This blog is not legal advice. It is a record of learning, reflection, and responsibility.
