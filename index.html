Earned Settlement: keeping momentum behind negotiated outcomes

Earned settlement is the discipline of proving that negotiated deals do more than end a dispute—they create a defensible runway for both sides to move forward. My current focus is on documenting each concession, translating headline terms into practical workflows, and safeguarding the relationship between counsel and client once the paper is signed.

This article-in-progress maps the lifecycle of earned settlement: rapid fact capture, financial modelling of options, drafting playbooks that align teams on messaging, and post-settlement monitoring so obligations never drift. Expect templates, checklists, and reflections on how technology can keep trust intact throughout the process.
